Paid event-queue submissions

Let viewers spend a currency to add an entry to the Timed Event Queue. Mods bypass the cost; if a mod rejects an entry (!teq reject), the viewer is refunded.

Viewers run !<name> your submission.
Define currencies on the Currencies page.
Passed to the queue as the entry's countdown when a mod accepts it.
